The Escale Legacy

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi family, Bella Vista began as a small trattoria with a simple dream: to bring authentic flavors to life.

Our recipes have been passed down through three generations, each adding their own touch while preserving the soul of our cuisine.

Today, we honor our roots while embracing innovation, sourcing the finest local ingredients to craft unforgettable dining experiences.
