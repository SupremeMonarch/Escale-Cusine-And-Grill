Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do I need a reservation? ⌄

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner service and weekends. You can book online through our reservations page or call us directly.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? ⌄

Absolutely! We offer gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when making your reservation or upon arrival.

Is there parking available? ⌄

Street parking is available in the area. We also have a parking spot in our premises for our customers to use.

Do you host private events? ⌄

Yes! We have private dining rooms available for special occasions, business meetings, and celebrations. Contact us for pricing and availability.

What's the dress code? ⌄

We maintain a smart casual dress code. While we don't require formal attire, we ask that guests dress in a way that won’t be uncomfortable to other customers
